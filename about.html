About The Company

What is BillingFox

Variable based pricing models have historically struggled, because of the technical limitations in the traditional financial systems. From authenticating bank accounts to monitoring the streaming flow of microtransactions has prevented the success of a viable alternative to ad based business models. However with the advent of blockchains and their ability to efficiently track minute payments down to fractions of a penny have opend the gateways for an explosion of commerce never before seen. BillingFox is the glue that binds new age blockchain models and the traditional banking system into a simple API call any developer can integrate into their video games, web apps, and software products.
