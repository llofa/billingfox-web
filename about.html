About The Company

What is BillingFox

Variable based pricing models have historically struggled, because of the technical limitations in the traditional financial systems. Authenticating bank accounts to monitoring the streaming flow of microtransactions has prevented the success of a viable alternative to ad based business models.

However with the advent of blockchains and their ability to efficiently track minute payments down to fractions of a penny have opend the gateways for an explosion of commerce never before seen. BillingFox is the glue that binds new age blockchain models and the traditional banking system into a simple API call any developer can integrate into their video games, web apps, and software products.

We're Just Getting Started

It starts with an idea

Tackling a problem as large as usage based billing is one that has been tackled by many before, however this time we get to harness the blockchain in revolutionzing microtransactions.

Early building days

After working with a hand full of developers to build out the initial prototype of the service, we were able to get a couple of those developers having a profitable running app sitting on top of the platform.

New endpoints and live network

Integrating the feedback from the early adopters into the API allowed us to really focus on providing value to our customers and by proxy their customers. Launching on the live Stellar network was the crowning acheivement to date.
