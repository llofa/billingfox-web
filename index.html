What is BillingFox?

A powerful API that enables billing cycles in units, seconds, minutes or days.

Ping

Test your access token and get some basic details on your account. Get information back such as the email used to sign up, referral code, and more.

Identify

Create users within the BillingFox ecosystem whose credits you can then begin to spend. Submit a user id, and the email associated with them in your system and your good to go.

Spend

Spend a user's account credit balance. Get the user id of the user and the amount of credits you want to charge the customer, and get back to building the core logic of your app, and not micro payments.

Recharge

Top up a user's account with a pre-determined amount of credits. Allow customers to charge up their account credits before they hit the low credit email alert.

Have Any Questions?

Who is BillingFox for?

Any software developer, company, or website looking to add the ability to charge their customers based on the usage of products, and/or services. With BillingFox at your disposal you can begin charging for API calls, seconds watched per video, minutes listened per podcast. Quite frankly the platform is built to identify an action and then charge credits against a user's account balance.

Why would I use BillingFox?

Variable pricing has historically been a challenge to pull off successfully due to a number of factors such as technical hurdles like backend processes, to the existing banking system, as well as unexpected consequences such as customer behavior. However with the explosion of social media, content marketing, and mobile devices the consumer's habits have changed and lend favorably to the future of usaged based business models.

What do I need to get started?

Not much to be honest with you, except for 2 things: an API Key from BillingFox, a Stripe Account, and finally some creativity to bring your ideas to life, with an untapped revenue model that many have yet to see. We guess the better question is what will you build?, and please show us when you're done, as we are as excited by the possibilities that a metered billing revenue model can bring.

What can I build on top of BillingFox?

Well the choice is yours really, you could build microtransaction into web, and mobile games. You could build a software license SaaS that bills developers for every software key generated. There is no limit to what can be built on top of the platform, as long as a user takes an action you can assign a value to it plain and simple.
